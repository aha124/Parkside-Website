# Product Requirement Document: Parkside Website

## 1. Introduction

**Purpose:** This document outlines the requirements for the website of Parkside, the Hershey Chapter of the Barbershop Harmony Society.

**Project Name:** `parkside-website`

## 2. Goals

*   Provide an online presence for the Parkside chapter.
*   Showcase the chapter's choruses: Parkside Harmony (men's) and Parkside Melody (treble).
*   Inform visitors about upcoming events.
*   Share news related to the chapter and choruses.
*   Provide information about joining or supporting the chapter.

## 3. Target Audience

*   Current members
*   Potential new members
*   Fans and supporters
*   Event organizers
*   General public interested in a cappella music

## 4. Core Features (Initial Observation)

*   Homepage with hero section (slideshow) and summaries of key sections.
*   Dedicated pages for each chorus (Parkside Harmony, Parkside Melody).
*   Events listing (potentially filterable).
*   News section.
*   Contact/Join information (Assumption: Likely needed).
*   About Us section (Assumption: Likely needed).
